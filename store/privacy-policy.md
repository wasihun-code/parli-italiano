# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026

Parla Italiano is designed to be privacy-first. The app's core conversation and training features work offline on your device and do not require you to create an account.

## Information We Collect

Parla Italiano does not collect personal data for its offline learning features. Conversation prompts and model responses are processed on your device.

If Firebase Analytics is enabled in a distributed build, it may collect anonymous usage and diagnostic information such as app opens, screen views, device type, crash signals, and general interaction events. This information is used only to understand app reliability and improve the learning experience.

## Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, precise location, payment information, or conversation content for the offline learning features.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Anonymous analytics data, if enabled, may be processed by Firebase/Google according to their service terms and privacy practices.

## Offline Use

The app is intended to work without an internet connection for its main learning experience. Some platform services, analytics, crash reporting, app updates, or store features may require network access.

## Children

Parla Italiano is a general language-learning app and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Updates should be published with the app or on the app's support page.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app developer or publisher through the support channel listed on the app store page.
