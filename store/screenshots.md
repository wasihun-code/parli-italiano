# Recommended Store Screenshots

Capture screenshots on a clean device or emulator with realistic sample progress and no personal information visible.

## 1. Home

Show the main learning dashboard with scenario progress visible. Highlight the breadth of the course and make sure the A1 beginner focus is clear from the visible content.

## 2. Vocabulary Training

Show a vocabulary or practice screen with Italian words and English meanings. Use a scenario with practical travel or daily-life terms.

## 3. Conversation

Show an active conversation practice screen with a short Italian exchange. Use a simple, friendly A1-level prompt such as ordering coffee or asking for directions.

## Tips

- Use both phone and tablet captures if the store listing supports them.
- Keep the status bar tidy and avoid notifications.
- Use high-contrast screens that are readable at thumbnail size.
- Capture the final release build so fonts, spacing, and native UI match production.
- Do not show API keys, debug menus, logs, or developer-only controls.
