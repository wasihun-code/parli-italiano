Parla Italiano helps beginners practice real Italian conversations offline, privately, and at their own pace.

Built for A1 learners, the app guides you through 110 everyday scenarios, from ordering coffee and asking for directions to shopping, travel, appointments, and simple social conversations. Each session combines useful vocabulary, focused prompts, and progressive training so you can build confidence one practical exchange at a time.

Practice conversation without needing a constant internet connection. The offline model runs on your device, making Parla Italiano useful while traveling, commuting, or studying anywhere.

Key features:
- Offline conversation practice for common Italian situations
- 110 practical A1-level scenarios
- Progressive training that builds from vocabulary to full exchanges
- Privacy-first design with on-device practice
- Simple lessons for travel, daily life, shopping, food, transport, and more

Parla Italiano is designed for learners who want practical speaking confidence, not memorized phrases alone.
